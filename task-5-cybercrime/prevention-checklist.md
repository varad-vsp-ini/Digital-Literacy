# Stay Safe Online: A Prevention Checklist for College Students

Navigating the digital world is essential for campus life, but it comes with risks. Use this checklist to audit your digital habits and stay protected against common cyber threats in India.

## 🛡️ General Digital Hygiene
- [ ] **Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Activate 2FA on your university email, WhatsApp, and social media accounts. Always prefer an Authenticator App over SMS-based OTPs when possible.
- [ ] **Audit App Permissions:** Periodically check your phone settings. Deny access to "Camera," "Microphone," or "Contacts" for apps that don’t strictly need them to function.
- [ ] **Use Strong, Unique Passwords:** Avoid using your name, birthdate, or "123456." Use a passphrase or a password manager to ensure every account has a different login.
- [ ] **Beware of Campus Wi-Fi:** Avoid accessing sensitive accounts or performing bank transactions on public or unprotected hostel Wi-Fi. Use a trusted VPN if you must connect.
- [ ] **Think Before You Click:** Be wary of shortened URLs in "Job Offer" or "Exam Results" emails. Hover over links to see the actual destination URL before clicking.
- [ ] **Secure Your Webcam:** Use a physical slider or a piece of tape to cover your laptop camera when not in use to prevent "camfecting" by malicious software.

## 💸 Financial & UPI Safety
- [ ] **PIN is for SENDING, not Receiving:** Remember that you **never** need to enter your UPI PIN to receive money. If someone asks you to enter your PIN to "claim a prize" or "receive a refund," it is a scam.
- [ ] **Verify the Merchant Name:** Before hitting 'Pay' on a UPI app, always double-check the verified name displayed on the screen. Scammers often use names similar to popular brands to deceive users.

---

## 🚨 If You Are Targeted
If you encounter a cybercrime or financial fraud, **speed is critical.** Report the incident immediately through the official Government of India channels:

* **Portal:** [cybercrime.gov.in](https://www.cybercrime.gov.in)
* **National Helpline:** **1930** (Available 24/7)
