### Part-B: Social Media Responsibility Checklist

| Do's | Don'ts |
| :--- | :--- |
| Audit your privacy settings regularly to know who can see your personal posts. | Post confidential information like student ID, home address, or private project details. |
| Use a professional profile picture and clear bio on LinkedIn to build a positive brand. | Engage in "cyber-venting" or posting complaints about professors, peers, or staff. |
| Verify information before sharing to prevent the spread of "fake news." | Plagiarize or share copyrighted material without proper attribution. |
| Network with industry professionals and alumni in relevant tech groups. | Post content depicting illegal activities or university policy violations. |
| Think before you tag others; respect the privacy of your friends and classmates. | Assume that "deleted" content is permanently gone from the internet. |
